About Us

Welcome to Al Atash Group

At Al Atash Group of Companies, we are committed to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our diverse portfolio spans across ten dynamic domains, each dedicated to delivering top-notch services and solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Domains

1. Real Estate:

Our real estate division offers comprehensive services in property development, management, sales, investments, rentals, and purchases. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, invest, or lease, we provide expert guidance and support to ensure seamless transactions and maximize investment value.

2. Architects & Associates:

Our architecture team brings visionary designs to life, creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces. We blend creativity with technical expertise to deliver innovative architectural solutions that meet our clients' unique requirements.

3. Estimation and Management:

With a team of seasoned professionals, our estimation and management services provide accurate project cost assessments, feasibility studies, and strategic advice. We help our clients make informed decisions, optimizing their projects for success.

4. Contrscter & Consultancy:

Al Atash Group's Contrscter & Consultancy division is synonymous with quality and precision. We undertake a wide range of construction projects, from residential buildings to commercial complexes, ensuring timely completion and adherence to the highest standards.

5. Land Survey:

Accurate land surveying is crucial for any development project. Our land survey division provides detailed topographical, boundary, and construction surveys, using the latest technology to deliver precise results.

6. Safety and Security:

Ensuring the safety and security of people and assets is paramount. Our safety and security division offers a range of services, including risk assessments, security solutions, and emergency preparedness plans, to protect what matters most.

7. MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing):

Our MEP division provides comprehensive mechanical, electrical, and plumbing services. From design and installation to maintenance, we ensure that all systems are efficient, reliable, and compliant with industry standards.

8. Media Power:

In the fast-paced world of media and communication, our media power division stands out by offering cutting-edge advertising, public relations, and digital marketing services. We help brands amplify their presence and engage with their target audience effectively.

9. Transport:

Our transport division ensures efficient and reliable transportation solutions, catering to both individuals and businesses. From logistics management to fleet services, we guarantee safe and timely deliveries, every time.

10. Buildgenices:

Buildgenices is our specialized arm focused on sustainable building practices and technologies. We promote eco-friendly construction methods and materials, contributing to a greener and more sustainable future.
